About me

Ma'ayan Shuvi

I am a graduated student in the CS visual computing group at Tel-Aviv University, where I work under supervision of Prof. Daniel Cohen-Or. I Obtained my B.Sc in Electrical Engineering from Tel-Aviv University. My research is focused on exploiting deep learning techniques to bridge the gap between images and video. I am experienced in optimization and processing of satellite images as an engineer in the Israeli Aeronautical Industry, and I served as an officer in the visual department of IDI. Furthermore, I spend my free time on photography and singing.
